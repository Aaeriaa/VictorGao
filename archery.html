Archery

Sep 2017 - Jun 2021

I had always wanted to try archery so in high school I joined the archery team, where I was given access to materials and taught how to shoot. Over the next months, I practiced and in April I went to the 2018 TDSSAA Archery City Championship. I was able to advance to the provincial competition and come in second place for the boy's standard division.

The next year, in addition to coming to practices, I helped manage and organize our inventory, teach new members, and at the start of the year, promote our team at the club fair.

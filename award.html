Canadian Open Mathematics Challenge (COMC) - A nationwide competition used to determine the Canadian Math Team. The top 125 students across Canada are invited to the CMO Qualifying Repêchage, of which the top 70 qualify for the Canadian Mathematical Olympiad (CMO).

  • 2019 COMC
  •     Top 125 (Invited to CMO Qualifying Repêchage)
  • 2018 COMC
  •     Top 125 (Invited to CMO Qualifying Repêchage)

American Mathematics Competition (AMC) - The national math competition for the USA. Out of over 100000 participants, the top 2.5% in the AMC 10 and top 5% in the AMC 12 are invited to compete in the AIME. The highest-scoring students then qualify for the USAMO.

  •     Top 50%
  • 2019 AMC 10B
  •     Top 2.5%
  • 2019 AMC 10A
  •     Top 2% (Invited to AIME)

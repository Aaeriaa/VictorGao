Swim Team

I don’t play many sports nowadays, but swimming will always be an exception. I’ve been swimming 4 or 5 times every week since I was 12 years old. In addition to getting exercise, a few times every year I go with my team to a swim meet.

Although I swim very often, I am yet to win any particularly significant medals or qualify for provincials. Still, the feeling you get when you put in all you’ve got, push on, and swim faster than you ever had - that's what makes it all worth it.

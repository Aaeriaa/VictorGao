Hydraulics Challenge

Feb 28 2016

In 2016 I and a team of 3 other students participated in the hydraulics science challenge at the Ontario science center. Over a few hours, we worked together and designed and built, using tubes, syringes, wooden sticks and glue, a working model of a hydraulic arm designed to remove concrete debris from roads and highways. At the end of the day, we tested our device to see that it worked.

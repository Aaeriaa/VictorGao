Swimming Instructor

Sept 2017 - Mar 2018

In the summer of 2017, I had been doing competitive swimming for a few years. My club also taught swimming lessons after our practices, so our coach asked some of us older kids to help teach. I had recently gotten the Bronze Cross lifeguard certificate and I was able to volunteer.

For the first week I followed an instructor, and the next week, I was assigned, my own class. For the first session, I taught a group of beginners, consisting of 6 children aged 4-8. I was given instructions and material to introduce but I was free to decide the lessons.
